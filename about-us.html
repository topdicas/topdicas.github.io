About-Us topdicas

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us. At topdicas, we follow these principles:

  • We do not ask for personal information unless it is truly necessary.
  • We do not share your personal information except to comply with the law, develop our products, or protect our rights.
  • We do not store personal information on our servers unless required for the ongoing operation of our site.

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, topdicas collects non-personally-identifying information, such as browser type, language preference, referring site, and date/time of requests. This information helps us understand how visitors use our site. We may release aggregated information, such as trends in site usage.

topdicas also collects potentially personally-identifying information, such as IP addresses for logged-in users and commenters, disclosed only as described in this policy.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain interactions with topdicas require personally-identifying information. For example, blog commenters provide a username and email address. We collect only the information necessary for the purpose of the interaction, and users can refuse to supply information, though this may limit access to certain features.

Protection of Personally-Identifying Information

topdicas does not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying information. We disclose such information only to employees, contractors, or as required by law. topdicas may email registered users about new features, feedback requests, or updates, but these communications are minimal. We take reasonable measures to protect this information against unauthorized access, use, alteration, or destruction.

Cookies

Cookies are used to track visitors and their preferences. Users who wish to disable cookies should adjust their browser settings, but some website features may not function properly without cookies.

External Links

topdicas may link to third-party websites. These sites may have privacy policies different from ours. We recommend reviewing their policies to understand how your information will be handled.

Ads

Ads on our site may use cookies set by advertising partners to deliver targeted content. This Privacy Policy applies to topdicas's use of cookies but not to those set by advertisers.

Comments

Comments processed by our anti-spam service are not stored on our servers unless flagged as false positives. These are kept temporarily to improve the service.

Children

We do not knowingly collect or target personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at miirak@monajat.org.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

topdicas may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Visitors should check this page regularly for updates. Continued use of the site after changes signifies acceptance of the revised policy.
